Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in the Solar System. Because the planet is so close to the sun, Mercury's surface temperature can reach a scorching 840 degrees Fahrenheit (450 degrees Celsius). Mercury is also the smallest planet. It is only slightly larger than Earth's moon and since it has no significant atmosphere to stop impacts, the planet is pockmarked with craters.
