The sun lies at the heart of the solar system, where it is by far the largest object. It holds 99.8 percent of the solar system's mass and is roughly 109 times the diameter of the Earth — about one million Earths could fit inside the sun. wikipedia

Mercury

Age:

4.503 billion years

Temperature:

-279F(-173C) Night to 801F(427C) Day

Mass:

3.3 × 10^23 kg

Diameter:

4879 kilometers

Atmosphere

Very thin to none at all

Orbital Period:

87 days

Surface Gravity:

37.7% of earth's gravity

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in the Solar System. Because the planet is so close to the sun, Mercury's surface temperature can reach a scorching 840 degrees Fahrenheit (450 degrees Celsius). Mercury is also the smallest planet. It is only slightly larger than Earth's moon and since it has no significant atmosphere to stop impacts, the planet is pockmarked with craters.
